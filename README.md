# Little Miss Witch

*A Python-based text adventure about brewing potions, discovering mysterious ingredients, and preparing for a dangerous journey.*

---

## Lore

You begin your journey on the day your apprenticeship comes to an abrupt end.

Your mentor, a renowned witch, departed on an expedition to the far western reaches of the world, into the mysterious Willow Creek Forests, seeking herbs said to possess extraordinary properties. Days turned into weeks, and weeks into months, until the inevitable news arrived.

She never returned.

With nothing but her workshop, her notes, and your determination, you inherit the role she left behind. Your brewing skills are still developing, your knowledge of alchemy is incomplete, and countless ingredients remain a mystery.

The path west is long, dangerous, and unforgiving. If you wish to uncover the truth behind your mentor's disappearance, you'll first have to become a witch worthy of the journey.

---

## Objective

Your primary goals are:

1. **Earn enough fortune** to fund an expedition to the distant western forests.
2. **Master the art of alchemy** by discovering ingredients, experimenting with recipes, and brewing increasingly powerful potions.
3. **Prepare for the journey ahead**, gathering the supplies and knowledge needed to survive whatever awaits beyond civilization.

---

## Current Status

This project is currently in active development.

The initial release focuses on building the core alchemy mechanics through a text-based interface. Once the gameplay systems are complete and polished, a graphical version is planned while reusing the same underlying game logic.
